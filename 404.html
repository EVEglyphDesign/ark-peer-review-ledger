Competition → collaboration

Goler argues that civilisations built on the competition game (“our team versus their team”) work only for limited stretches — “maybe 50 years tops” — because competitive systems incentivise removing rivals “from the board.” The shift to a collaboration game is the next-stage move.

Danny Goler, ~1:03:06+.

This is the exact posture already written into the canon as Non-Disruption: EVE Glyph deliberately takes the opposite position of incumbents without aiming to disrupt or displace them; incumbents retain their operational role; the sovereign stack is aimed at vulnerable people and organisations that cannot afford the commercial equivalent. Collaboration is not the aspiration — it is the operating clause.
